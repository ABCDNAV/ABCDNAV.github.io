Jalebi is a beloved South Asian dessert, featuring coiled, deep-fried batter soaked in fragrant sugar syrup. With a crispy-sweet texture, adorned with nuts and spices, it embodies South Asian culinary traditions.

Gulab Jamun, a cherished South Asian dessert, features soft, golden-brown dumplings made from milk solids. Deep-fried to perfection and soaked in fragrant cardamom or rose-infused syrup, it's a sweet delight adored at celebrations.

$24.99 per kg

Gajrela Barfi, a South Asian delight, fuses carrot pudding (gajar ka halwa) with milk-based barfi. Grated carrots simmered with milk, sugar, and spices result in a creamy confection, blending carrot's sweetness with barfi's richness.

Milk Cake is a cherished South Asian sweet, known for its simplicity and rich flavor. Made by condensing milk and sugar until it thickens, it offers a creamy, slightly crumbly texture.
